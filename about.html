About Auroria Isles

Hidden beyond the clouds and nestled in the sky-blue sea, Auroria Isles is a mythical archipelago where time slows down, and magic flourishes. Once believed to be the home of celestial dolphins, this island is known for its glowing sands, floating lantern forests, and skywater falls that shimmer under moonlight.
